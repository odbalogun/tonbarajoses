Strategic Consultant

Clear Communication. Stronger Culture. Better Reputation.

I work with organisations and senior leaders to improve how they communicate — internally and externally.

What I Help With

1. Executive Presence & Media Confidence

I work with senior leaders and spokespeople who operate in high-visibility or high-pressure environments. I help them:

  • Communicate clearly and confidently
  • Strengthen their executive presence
  • Prepare for media interviews and public appearances
  • Manage perception in sensitive situations

Leaders represent the organisation — how they show up matters.

2. DE&I Strategy That Is Credible

DE&I should not feel performative or disconnected from reality. I support organisations to:

  • Align DE&I messaging with actual internal practices
  • Identify gaps between culture and communication
  • Refine leadership language around inclusion
  • Build narratives that are both culturally intelligent and commercially sound

The goal is authenticity and long-term credibility — not optics.

3. Internal Communications & Culture Alignment

Organisations function better when communication is clear and consistent. I help teams:

  • Review internal communication structures
  • Clarify leadership messaging and tone
  • Reduce confusion caused by mixed signals
  • Strengthen employee trust through transparency

Clear communication improves morale, retention, and overall performance.

4. External Messaging & Reputation Management

External messaging should be strategic — not reactive. Drawing on my broadcast background, I advise on:

  • Corporate storytelling and positioning
  • Media readiness
  • Crisis-aware communication
  • Protecting and strengthening reputation

The aim is sustainable credibility, not short-term visibility.

Speaking & Advisory Engagements

Alongside my consultancy work, I speak at corporate events, leadership forums, and private executive sessions. My talks focus on:

  • Media, culture, and commercial storytelling
  • Executive presence in high-visibility environments
  • Women, power, and perception
  • Reputation intelligence in the digital era
  • Building culturally intelligent brands

Each session is practical, strategic, and grounded in real-world media and leadership experience.

For advisory, media, or speaking enquiries, please get in touch directly via email.

This is a bespoke, one-to-one advisory package designed for senior leaders operating in corporate, government-adjacent, or high-scrutiny environments. It is a structured, private engagement tailored to your specific role, visibility level, and organisational context.

The programme may include:

  • Executive presence refinement
  • Public image and perception strategy

This is high-level, discreet strategic support for leaders who understand that visibility requires preparation.
