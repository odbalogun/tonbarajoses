About Tonbara Joses

I am a senior Television Director and Media Communications leader with over 18 years of experience delivering live broadcast, commercial content, and culturally relevant storytelling across retail and media platforms.

I have led and directed complex, multi-camera live productions spanning fashion, beauty, lifestyle, panel discussions, and award-style programming. My work focuses on translating editorial and commercial objectives into clear, engaging on-air narratives that connect with audiences and drive results. I am trusted for calm, decisive leadership in high-pressure live environments and for maintaining consistently high broadcast standards.

My expertise spans live directing, vision mixing, broadcast operations, and editorial leadership, supported by a strong arts and design foundation. I work closely with producers, presenters, commercial teams, and executives to shape content that is both creatively compelling and commercially effective.

Alongside broadcast, I operate at the intersection of media, culture, and communications. As an Associate Editor and DE&I leader, I help shape inclusive narratives, influence content strategy, and apply a communications lens to business decisions, audience growth, and brand positioning. I am particularly interested in how media can reflect culture responsibly while remaining commercially relevant.

I hold a qualification in Broadcast Operations and Production (Radio & Television) from Ravensbourne College of Design and Communication.

What I Do

Multifaceted professional with diverse expertise

Broadcaster and Media Specialist

With over 17 years of experience in television direction, I've had the privilege of shaping narratives that resonate with diverse audiences across the globe. My journey in the creative industry has been defined by a passion for authentic storytelling and a commitment to excellence.

As a broadcaster and media specialist, I bring a unique perspective to television production, combining technical expertise with creative vision to deliver compelling content that engages and inspires viewers.

Consultant

I provide expert consultation in media training and management, helping organizations and individuals develop their broadcast capabilities and leadership skills in the media industry.

My consulting services focus on strategic media planning, content development, and team management, empowering clients to achieve their communication goals effectively.

CEO Founder of Melora

As the CEO and Founder of Melora, I'm leading a revolution in the beauty and cosmetics industry by redefining access to cosmetic products for consumers worldwide.

Melora is committed to making high-quality cosmetic products more accessible, inclusive, and affordable, while maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety.
